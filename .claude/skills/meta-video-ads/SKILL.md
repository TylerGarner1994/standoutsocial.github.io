# Meta Video Ads Skill

## Activation
Use this skill when: Writing video ad scripts for Facebook and Instagram, creating UGC-style video concepts, developing VSL-lite scripts for paid social.

**Activate with**: "Write video ad scripts", "Create Meta video ads", "UGC ad scripts"

## Video Ad Fundamentals

### The Numbers That Matter
- **3-second view rate**: Target >25% (how many watch past the first 3 seconds)
- **ThruPlay rate**: Target >15% (how many watch to 15 seconds or completion)
- **Average watch time**: Benchmark against your own account
- **Hook rate**: First 3 seconds determines everything

### Video Length Guidelines
- **Story/Reel ads**: 5-15 seconds (swipe culture)
- **Feed ads**: 15-30 seconds (optimal range)
- **Performance video ads**: 30-60 seconds (when hook is strong)
- **VSL-lite**: 90-180 seconds (for warm audiences only)

## The Hook Formula (First 3 Seconds)

The hook must do ONE thing: prevent the thumb from moving.

### Hook Types
**Visual hook**: Something unexpected happens on screen
**Audio hook**: Surprising sound, music change, or statement
**Text hook**: Bold statement on screen before voice starts
**Pattern interrupt**: Something doesn't compute — creates curiosity

### 7 Proven Hook Structures

1. **The Bold Claim**:
"I [achieved impressive result] in [timeframe] by doing [unusual thing]."

2. **The Controversial Opinion**:
"[Common thing] is actually [counterintuitive claim]. Here's proof."

3. **The Direct Problem**:
"If you're [specific experience], this is for you."

4. **The Curiosity Gap**:
"Nobody talks about [thing]. But it [changed/explains/fixes] everything."

5. **The Result First**:
"[Person] went from [bad state] to [good state]. Here's exactly how."

6. **The Challenge**:
"Try [specific action] for [timeframe]. I guarantee [result]."

7. **The Question**:
"Why do [some group] always [achieve result] while [other group] [fail]?"

## Script Structure Templates

### Template 1: The 15-Second Punch
```
[0-3s] HOOK: Bold claim or direct problem statement
[3-8s] AGITATE or TEASE: Expand briefly or preview what's coming
[8-13s] SOLUTION: Quick reveal of what/who
[13-15s] CTA: Single clear action
```

### Template 2: The 30-Second Story
```
[0-3s] HOOK: Result-first or problem identification
[3-8s] PROBLEM: Briefly establish the struggle
[8-18s] SOLUTION: Introduce what changed
[18-25s] PROOF: Quick social proof or result
[25-30s] CTA: Clear action instruction
```

### Template 3: The 60-Second Deep Sell
```
[0-3s] HOOK: Pattern interrupt or bold claim
[3-10s] PROBLEM: Agitate the pain
[10-20s] MECHANISM: Introduce the solution approach
[20-35s] PROOF: Testimonial or result showcase
[35-50s] OFFER: What they get + why now
[50-60s] CTA: Specific instruction + urgency
```

### Template 4: UGC-Style (Authentic)
```
[0-3s] Natural hook: Casual delivery, direct to camera
[3-15s] Personal story: "I used to [problem] until..."
[15-30s] Discovery: "Then I found/tried [thing]"
[30-45s] Results: "Here's what happened..."
[45-55s] Recommendation: "If you're [situation], you need to try this"
[55-60s] CTA: Where to find it
```

```markdown

**Length**: [X seconds]
**Format**: [Feed/Story/Reel/UGC]
**Audience**: [Cold/Warm + description]

---

### VISUAL DIRECTION
[What's happening on screen — setting, person, action]

### SCRIPT

**[0-3s] HOOK**
*[Visual note]*
[SPOKEN]: "[Exact words]"
*[Text overlay if any]*

**[3-10s] BODY**
*[Visual note]*
[SPOKEN]: "[Exact words]"

**[10-25s] CORE MESSAGE**
*[Visual note]*
[SPOKEN]: "[Exact words]"

**[25-30s] CTA**
*[Visual note]*
[SPOKEN]: "[Exact words]"
*[Text overlay CTA]*

---

### PRODUCTION NOTES
- Talent direction: [How they should deliver]
- Key shot: [Most important visual moment]
- B-roll suggestions: [Supporting footage]
- Music mood: [Type of background music]

### WHY THIS SHOULD WORK
[1-2 sentences on the psychological driver]
```

## UGC Brief Template
When briefing creators for authentic UGC-style content:

```
## UGC Brief: [Product/Campaign]

**What we're making**: [Format + length]
**Your character**: [Natural self / customer who found this / someone sharing a tip]
**Setting**: [Home/outdoor/workplace — should feel real, not staged]

**Required hook (say this first)**:
"[Exact hook or approved option]"

**Story to tell** (in your own words, not scripted):
1. [Point 1 to cover]
2. [Point 2 to cover]
3. [Point 3 to cover]

**Must include**:
- [Claim or statement that must appear]
- [Product name/mention]
- [CTA]

**Tone**: [Conversational, excited, matter-of-fact, etc.]

**Do NOT**:
- [Specific things to avoid]
- Sound scripted
- [Platform compliance notes]
```

## Compliance Notes
- UGC creators must disclose if they received product/compensation
- No before/after for health products
- Results claims need "results may vary" qualifier
- Platform review: Instagram Story ads often reviewed more strictly
