# Persona Architect Skill

## Activation
Use this skill when: Building detailed customer personas for marketing, understanding multiple audience segments, informing ad creative and copy.

**Activate with**: "Build customer personas", "Create audience avatars", "Develop persona profiles"

## Persona Development Framework

### Input Requirements
To build accurate personas, gather:
- Product/service description
- Target market (rough idea is fine)
- Any existing customer data or feedback
- Who the client thinks their customer is
- Competitor research if available

### Persona Depth Levels

**Level 1: Quick Persona** (5 minutes)
For rapid creative decisions:
- Age range, gender, income bracket
- Primary pain point (1 sentence)
- Primary desire (1 sentence)
- Language they use

**Level 2: Standard Persona** (15-20 minutes)
For campaign planning:
- Full demographics
- Psychographic profile
- Pain and desire stacks
- Media consumption habits
- Buying triggers and barriers

**Level 3: Deep Persona** (30-45 minutes)
For major campaigns or new market entry:
- Full Level 2 details
- Day-in-the-life narrative
- Relationship with the problem
- Failed solution history
- Identity and self-perception
- Decision-making style

## Integration Notes
- Feed personas into all creative briefs
- Use language guide sections for direct copy integration
- Creative implications section → directly informs ad angle selection
- Objections section → informs FAQ and guarantee copy
- Share with client before creative development begins
