# Brand Extraction Skill

## Activation
Use this skill when: You have a website URL or existing content and need to quickly extract brand voice, tone, and style guidelines.

**Activate with**: "Extract brand voice", "Analyse this website's style", "What's their brand tone?"

## Quick Brand Analysis Framework

When given a URL or content samples, extract:

### 1. Voice Characteristics
Analyse and document:
- **Formality level**: Casual ↔ Professional ↔ Corporate
- **Personality**: Friendly, authoritative, playful, serious, inspiring, practical
- **Energy**: High energy, calm, urgent, measured
- **Perspective**: We/Us focused, You focused, I/personal

### 2. Tone Markers
Identify specific language patterns:
- Vocabulary level (simple vs. sophisticated)
- Sentence structure (short punchy vs. longer explanatory)
- Punctuation style (exclamation use, em dashes, ellipses)
- Australian/British/American English

### 3. Key Messages
Extract:
- Core promise/proposition
- Primary differentiators
- Repeated themes or values
- Avoided topics or language

### 4. Customer Language
Note how they talk about:
- Their customers (clients, members, people, businesses)
- The problem they solve
- The solution they provide
- Results they promise

- [Specific guidance]

## Quick Application
After extraction, use this profile to:
1. Check all content against voice characteristics
2. Rewrite generic copy in brand voice
3. Create new content that feels native to the brand
4. Train on specific vocabulary and phrasing
